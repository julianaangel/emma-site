Multiple Scattering Calculation


The angular distribution after scattering is evaluated with a modified NSW model detailed in "Multiple Scattering of Charged Particles" by Marion and Zimmerman, 1966. A Gaussian (normalized to unity at angle=0) is fit to the model distribution such that it has the same width as the distribution at the 1/e point. Integrals and functions are solved numerically with Python.
